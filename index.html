Good questions

Sniff out the details.

What makes the ingredients "better"?

Every treat uses single-source proteins, real fruits and vegetables, and no artificial fillers, dyes, or by-product meals. If we wouldn't feel good feeding it to our own dogs, it doesn't go in the box.

Can I customize for allergies or size?

Yes. When you sign up you tell us your dog's size, age, and any sensitivities, and we match treats and toy durability accordingly. You can update this anytime from your account.

Can I skip or cancel?

Anytime, no fees. Manage, skip, or cancel from your account before your monthly cutoff and you won't be charged for the next box.

When will my box ship?

Boxes ship within the first week of each month. Order before the monthly cutoff to get the current themed box.

Do you ship outside the US?

We currently ship across the US and Canada, with more regions on the way. Join the newsletter to hear when we reach yours.
