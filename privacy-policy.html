Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sprig Co. ("Sprig," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or subscribe to our service. By using Sprig, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account & order details: your name, email, shipping address, and the plan and box preferences you choose.
  • Dog profile: information you give us about your dog, such as size, age, and any allergies or sensitivities, so we can tailor your box.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: basic analytics about how you use our site, collected to help us improve it.

How we use your information

  • To fulfill and deliver your subscription boxes.
  • To personalize your boxes to your dog.
  • To communicate about your account, orders, and support requests.
  • To send marketing messages, which you can opt out of at any time.
  • To improve our products, website, and service.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate — such as payment processors, shipping carriers, and email tools — and only as needed to provide our service, or where required by law.

Your choices

You can review or update your account information, opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link, and request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work without them.

Questions about your privacy? Email us at privacy@sprig.example and we will be glad to help.
