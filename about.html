Sprig started with a simple frustration: too many treats hide cheap fillers behind cute packaging. So we built a box we'd actually trust.

Our Story

It began with one picky rescue.

When our founder adopted a sensitive-stomached rescue named Sprig, every store-bought treat seemed to cause trouble — long ingredient lists, mystery "meat meal," dyes for the humans more than the dogs.

So we started making treats at home from single-source proteins and real produce, and roping in a vet to vet every recipe. Friends' dogs wanted in. That kitchen experiment became the box you see today: better ingredients, tougher toys, delivered every month.

Our promise

No fillers. No mystery meat. No dyes. A licensed vet signs off on every single box before it ships to your door.
